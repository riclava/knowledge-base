## 学习总原则

### 1. 语法的本质

英语语法可以理解为四层系统：

| 层级 | 解决的问题 | 例子 |
|---|---|---|
| 词类 | 每个词能做什么 | noun, verb, adjective, preposition |
| 句子成分 | 词组在句子里承担什么功能 | subject, object, complement, modifier |
| 结构关系 | 信息如何连接和嵌套 | coordination, subordination, relative clause |
| 意义选择 | 说话者如何表达时间、态度、重点 | tense, aspect, voice, mood, modality, emphasis |

一句话概括：词组成短语，短语组成句子成分，句子成分组成句子，句子再组成篇章。

### 2. 最有效的学习顺序

建议按下面顺序学：

1. 句子五大基本句型
2. 名词、动词、形容词、副词、介词、连词
3. 动词时态、语态、情态、非谓语
4. 名词性从句、定语从句、状语从句
5. 倒装、强调、省略、虚拟语气、间接引语
6. 标点、篇章衔接、正式写作风格

不要先背所有术语。先学会识别句子主干：谁做什么、谁是什么、谁对谁做了什么。

### 3. 读句子的五个问题

遇到长句，按这五步拆：

1. 谓语动词在哪里？
2. 主语是谁？
3. 宾语、补语或表语是什么？
4. 修饰成分修饰谁？
5. 从句从哪里开始，到哪里结束，充当什么成分？

例句：

> The report that the team submitted after the audit shows that the new workflow can reduce errors.

拆解：

- 主句主语：The report
- 定语从句：that the team submitted after the audit，修饰 report
- 主句谓语：shows
- 宾语从句：that the new workflow can reduce errors

## 英语句子的核心模型

### 1. 句子最小核心

英语完整句通常需要：

```text
Subject + Predicate
主语 + 谓语
```

例子：

- Birds fly.
- She smiled.
- The system failed.

谓语必须包含限定动词，也就是会随着时态、人称、数发生变化的动词形式。

### 2. 五大基本句型

| 句型 | 结构 | 说明 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|---|
| SV | 主语 + 不及物动词 | 动作不需要宾语 | The baby cried. |
| SVC | 主语 + 系动词 + 表语 | 表示主语是什么或怎么样 | The plan seems practical. |
| SVO | 主语 + 及物动词 + 宾语 | 动作作用于对象 | She fixed the bug. |
| SVOO | 主语 + 动词 + 间宾 + 直宾 | 给某人某物 | He sent me a message. |
| SVOC | 主语 + 动词 + 宾语 + 宾补 | 让宾语变成某状态 | They made the room quiet. |

学习任何复杂句，都先还原到这五种骨架。

### 3. 句子成分总表

| 成分 | 英文 | 功能 | 常见形式 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 主语 | subject | 动作发出者或句子谈论对象 | 名词、代词、动名词、不定式、从句 |
| 谓语 | predicate / verb phrase | 说明主语的动作、状态、关系 | 动词短语 |
| 宾语 | object | 动作承受者 | 名词、代词、动名词、不定式、从句 |
| 表语 | subject complement | 说明主语身份或状态 | 名词、形容词、介词短语、从句 |
| 宾补 | object complement | 补充说明宾语 | 名词、形容词、分词、不定式 |
| 定语 | attributive / modifier | 修饰名词 | 形容词、名词、介词短语、分词、定语从句 |
| 状语 | adverbial | 修饰动词、形容词、副词或全句 | 副词、介词短语、不定式、分词、状语从句 |
| 同位语 | appositive | 对名词作解释说明 | 名词短语、从句 |

### 4. 短语与从句

| 单位 | 特点 | 例子 |
|---|---|---|
| word | 单个词 | useful |
| phrase | 没有主谓结构的词组 | a useful tool |
| clause | 有主谓结构 | when the tool is ready |
| sentence | 能独立表达完整意思 | The tool is ready. |

短语常见类型：

- noun phrase: the new feature
- verb phrase: has been tested
- adjective phrase: very easy to use
- adverb phrase: quite carefully
- prepositional phrase: in the folder
- infinitive phrase: to solve the problem
- participle phrase: built for beginners
- gerund phrase: learning grammar systematically

## 词类系统

### 1. 八大词类

| 词类 | 英文 | 核心功能 | 例子 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 名词 | noun | 表示人、物、地点、概念 | student, app, London, freedom |
| 代词 | pronoun | 代替名词 | I, you, it, this, who |
| 动词 | verb | 表示动作、状态、关系 | run, know, be, become |
| 形容词 | adjective | 修饰名词或作表语 | useful, clear, expensive |
| 副词 | adverb | 修饰动词、形容词、副词或全句 | quickly, very, fortunately |
| 介词 | preposition | 引出名词性成分，表示关系 | in, on, at, by, for |
| 连词 | conjunction | 连接词、短语、从句、句子 | and, but, because, although |
| 感叹词 | interjection | 表示感情或反应 | oh, wow, hey |

### 2. 词类和句子成分不要混淆

词类是词本身的类型；句子成分是它在句子里的工作。

例子：

- Running is healthy.  
  Running 是动名词，作主语。
- I like running.  
  running 是动名词，作宾语。
- The running water is cold.  
  running 是现在分词形容词，作定语。

同一个形式，在不同位置可能承担不同功能。

## 名词与限定词

### 1. 名词类型

| 类型 | 说明 | 例子 |
|---|---|---|
| 普通名词 | 一类人或物 | book, city, engineer |
| 专有名词 | 特定名称，通常大写 | China, Microsoft, Monday |
| 可数名词 | 可以数，有单复数 | a file, two files |
| 不可数名词 | 不能直接数 | water, advice, information |
| 集合名词 | 一组人或物 | team, family, audience |
| 抽象名词 | 概念、品质、状态 | honesty, growth, risk |
| 复合名词 | 两个或多个词构成 | user guide, login page |

### 2. 可数与不可数

| 可数名词 | 不可数名词 |
|---|---|
| a question, many questions | advice, much advice |
| a problem, several problems | information, some information |
| a machine, two machines | equipment, a piece of equipment |

高频不可数名词：

- advice
- information
- knowledge
- furniture
- equipment
- luggage / baggage
- progress
- research
- news
- software
- traffic
- weather
- evidence

如果要数不可数名词，常用量词：

- a piece of advice
- a piece of information
- a cup of coffee
- a bottle of water
- an item of furniture

### 3. 名词复数

| 规则 | 形式 | 例子 |
|---|---|---|
| 一般加 -s | noun + s | book -> books |
| s, x, ch, sh 结尾加 -es | noun + es | box -> boxes |
| 辅音 + y 变 -ies | y -> ies | city -> cities |
| 元音 + y 加 -s | + s | boy -> boys |
| f / fe 常变 -ves | f -> ves | leaf -> leaves |
| 不规则 | 特殊变化 | child -> children, mouse -> mice |
| 单复同形 | 不变 | sheep, deer, species |

外来词复数在现代英语中常有两种：

- analysis -> analyses
- criterion -> criteria
- datum -> data，但 data 在现代英语中常作集合名词使用

### 4. 所有格

| 形式 | 用法 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 's | 有生命名词、时间、机构 | Sarah's laptop; today's meeting |
| ' | 复数名词以 s 结尾 | the users' feedback |
| of | 无生命物、正式关系 | the title of the book |
| 双重所有格 | of + 名词性物主代词 / 's | a friend of mine |

注意：

- my father's car = 父亲的车
- a photo of my father = 照片内容是父亲
- a photo of my father's = 父亲拥有的一张照片

### 5. 限定词

限定词放在名词前，告诉读者这个名词的范围、数量、所属或特指程度。

| 类型 | 例子 |
|---|---|
| 冠词 | a, an, the |
| 指示限定词 | this, that, these, those |
| 物主限定词 | my, your, his, her, its, our, their |
| 数量限定词 | some, any, many, much, few, little, several |
| 数词 | one, two, first, second |
| 不定限定词 | each, every, either, neither, all, both |

### 6. 冠词 a / an / the / 零冠词

| 形式 | 核心含义 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| a / an | 一个；第一次提到；非特指 | I saw a dog. |
| the | 这个；双方都知道；特指 | The dog was barking. |
| 零冠词 | 泛指复数、不可数、专有名词等 | Dogs are friendly. Water is essential. |

使用逻辑：

1. 单数可数名词通常不能裸奔：a book / the book / my book。
2. 第一次提到常用 a / an，第二次提到常用 the。
3. 表示一类事物时，可用复数裸名词或不可数裸名词：Books can change people.
4. 独一无二事物常用 the：the sun, the earth, the internet。
5. 国家、城市、人名通常零冠词：China, Beijing, Alice。
6. 山脉、河流、海洋、群岛常用 the：the Alps, the Nile, the Pacific。

### 7. 限定词顺序

常见顺序：

```text
前限定词 + 中限定词 + 后限定词 + 形容词 + 名词
all / both / half + the / my / this + three / many + useful + tools
```

例子：

- all the three old books
- both my two younger brothers
- many useful learning resources

## 代词

### 1. 人称代词

| 人称 | 主格 | 宾格 | 形容词性物主 | 名词性物主 | 反身代词 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 第一人称单数 | I | me | my | mine | myself |
| 第二人称 | you | you | your | yours | yourself / yourselves |
| 第三人称单数阳性 | he | him | his | his | himself |
| 第三人称单数阴性 | she | her | her | hers | herself |
| 第三人称单数中性 | it | it | its | its | itself |
| 第一人称复数 | we | us | our | ours | ourselves |
| 第三人称复数 | they | them | their | theirs | themselves |

例子：

- She helped me.
- This is my book. The book is mine.
- They introduced themselves.

### 2. 指示代词

| 单数 | 复数 | 距离 |
|---|---|---|
| this | these | 近 |
| that | those | 远 |

例子：

- This is useful.
- Those are outdated.

### 3. 不定代词

| 类型 | 例子 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| 人 | someone, anyone, everyone, no one | 通常作单数 |
| 物 | something, anything, everything, nothing | 通常作单数 |
| 数量 | some, any, many, much, few, little | 根据名词可数性选择 |
| 个体 | each, every, either, neither | 通常接单数 |
| 全体 | all, both | 根据语境决定 |

例子：

- Everyone is ready.
- Both are correct.
- Few people noticed the change.
- Little progress was made.

### 4. it 的核心用法

| 用法 | 例句 |
|---|---|
| 指代前文事物 | I bought a laptop. It is fast. |
| 指天气、时间、距离 | It is raining. It is late. It is ten miles away. |
| 形式主语 | It is important to test the code. |
| 形式宾语 | I found it hard to explain. |
| 强调句 | It was John who solved the issue. |

### 5. there be 句型

there be 用来引入新信息，表示某处存在某物。

- There is a problem.
- There are three options.
- There seems to be a delay.

注意主谓一致通常看 be 后面的名词：

- There is a book and two pens on the desk.
- There are two pens and a book on the desk.

### 6. 关系代词与疑问代词

| 类型 | 代词 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 疑问代词 | who, whom, whose, what, which | Who called you? |
| 关系代词 | who, whom, whose, which, that | The user who reported the bug is online. |

## 形容词与副词

### 1. 形容词的功能

| 功能 | 位置 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 定语 | 名词前 | a clear explanation |
| 表语 | 系动词后 | The explanation is clear. |
| 宾补 | 宾语后 | Keep the door open. |
| 后置定语 | 不定代词后、固定结构中 | something useful; the best way possible |

### 2. 形容词顺序

多个形容词修饰名词时，常见顺序：

```text
限定词 + 评价 + 大小 + 年龄 + 形状 + 颜色 + 来源 + 材料 + 用途 + 名词
```

例子：

- a beautiful small old round red Italian wooden dining table

实际写作中不要堆太多形容词；超过三个通常显得笨重。

### 3. 比较级和最高级

| 形式 | 构成 | 例子 |
|---|---|---|
| 单音节 | -er / -est | fast, faster, fastest |
| 辅元辅重读 | 双写尾字母 + er / est | big, bigger, biggest |
| 辅音 + y | y -> ier / iest | easy, easier, easiest |
| 多音节 | more / most | useful, more useful, most useful |
| 不规则 | 特殊变化 | good, better, best |

常用结构：

- as ... as: This tool is as reliable as the old one.
- not as / so ... as: It is not as simple as it looks.
- 比较级 + than: This version is faster than the last one.
- the + 比较级, the + 比较级: The earlier you start, the better the result.
- 比较级 and 比较级: The task is getting harder and harder.

### 4. 副词的功能

| 类型 | 说明 | 例子 |
|---|---|---|
| 方式副词 | 动作如何发生 | carefully, quickly |
| 时间副词 | 何时 | now, then, recently |
| 地点副词 | 何地 | here, there, nearby |
| 频率副词 | 多常发生 | always, often, rarely |
| 程度副词 | 程度 | very, quite, almost |
| 连接副词 | 句间关系 | however, therefore, meanwhile |
| 句子副词 | 说话者态度 | fortunately, honestly, clearly |

### 5. 副词位置

| 副词类型 | 常见位置 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 频率副词 | be 后、实义动词前、助动词后 | She often writes. She is always kind. |
| 方式副词 | 动词后或宾语后 | He explained the rule clearly. |
| 程度副词 | 被修饰词前 | very useful, almost finished |
| 连接副词 | 句首、句中或句末 | However, the result changed. |

注意：

- hard 是形容词或副词，hardly 是“几乎不”。
- late 是形容词或副词，lately 是“最近”。
- high 是具体高度，highly 是抽象程度：highly recommended。

## 介词

### 1. 介词的核心

介词连接名词性成分，表达时间、空间、原因、方式、对象、主题等关系。

结构：

```text
preposition + object
介词 + 宾语
```

例子：

- in the room
- on Monday
- by email
- for beginners
- with confidence

### 2. 时间介词

| 介词 | 用法 | 例子 |
|---|---|---|
| at | 具体时刻、节日点 | at 8:00, at noon, at Christmas |
| on | 具体日期、星期、某天上下午 | on Monday, on April 26, on Friday morning |
| in | 月份、年份、季节、较长时间 | in 2026, in spring, in the morning |
| for | 持续多久 | for three years |
| since | 自从某时起 | since 2020 |
| during | 在某段期间内 | during the meeting |
| by | 截止到某时 | by Friday |
| until / till | 直到某时 | until midnight |
| from ... to ... | 从某时到某时 | from 9 to 5 |

### 3. 空间介词

| 介词 | 核心含义 | 例子 |
|---|---|---|
| in | 在内部 | in the box |
| on | 在表面 | on the table |
| at | 在某点 | at the door |
| over | 在上方，跨越 | over the bridge |
| above | 高于 | above sea level |
| under | 正下方 | under the bed |
| below | 低于 | below zero |
| between | 两者之间 | between A and B |
| among | 三者或以上之间 | among the students |
| across | 横穿表面 | across the street |
| through | 穿过内部 | through the tunnel |
| along | 沿着 | along the river |
| into | 进入 | into the room |
| out of | 从里面出来 | out of the box |

### 4. 抽象介词

| 搭配 | 例句 |
|---|---|
| interested in | She is interested in linguistics. |
| good at | He is good at explaining grammar. |
| responsible for | The team is responsible for testing. |
| similar to | This rule is similar to the previous one. |
| different from | This version is different from the old one. |
| depend on | The result depends on the data. |
| focus on | We focus on clarity. |
| based on | The decision is based on evidence. |

介词最难靠规则完全推出，应该用“动词 / 形容词 / 名词 + 介词”的搭配来记。

## 连词与连接结构

### 1. 并列连词

连接语法地位相同的成分。

| 连词 | 关系 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| and | 并列、递进 | She reads and writes every day. |
| but | 转折 | The rule is simple, but the usage is subtle. |
| or | 选择 | You can revise it or rewrite it. |
| nor | 否定并列 | He did not call, nor did he email. |
| for | 原因，正式 | The road was closed, for it was flooded. |
| so | 结果 | It rained, so we stayed home. |
| yet | 转折 | The task is hard, yet possible. |

### 2. 从属连词

引导从句，让从句依附于主句。

| 类型 | 连词 |
|---|---|
| 时间 | when, while, before, after, until, since, as soon as |
| 原因 | because, since, as |
| 条件 | if, unless, provided that, as long as |
| 让步 | although, though, even though, while |
| 目的 | so that, in order that |
| 结果 | so ... that, such ... that |
| 比较 | than, as ... as |
| 方式 | as, as if, as though |

### 3. 关联连词

| 结构 | 例句 |
|---|---|
| both ... and | Both speed and accuracy matter. |
| either ... or | Either option is acceptable. |
| neither ... nor | Neither the code nor the docs are ready. |
| not only ... but also | She not only writes clearly but also teaches well. |
| whether ... or | I do not know whether he will stay or leave. |

注意平行结构：

- Good: She likes reading, writing, and teaching.
- Poor: She likes reading, writing, and to teach.

## 动词总系统

动词是英语语法的中心。掌握动词，就掌握了句子的时间、结构和态度。

### 1. 动词分类

| 分类角度 | 类型 | 例子 |
|---|---|---|
| 是否作谓语 | 限定动词 / 非限定动词 | writes / to write, writing, written |
| 是否需要宾语 | 及物 / 不及物 | read a book / sleep |
| 是否表示状态 | 动态 / 静态 | run / know |
| 是否连接表语 | 系动词 | be, seem, become, feel |
| 是否帮助构成语法 | 助动词 | be, have, do |
| 是否表达态度 | 情态动词 | can, may, must, should |
| 是否带小品词 | 短语动词 | give up, look after |

### 2. 实义动词、助动词、情态动词

| 类型 | 功能 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 实义动词 | 表示主要动作或状态 | She writes. |
| 助动词 be | 构成进行时、被动语态 | She is writing. It was written. |
| 助动词 have | 构成完成时 | She has written it. |
| 助动词 do | 构成疑问、否定、强调 | Do you agree? I do agree. |
| 情态动词 | 表达能力、可能、义务、建议 | You should revise it. |

### 3. 动词五种基本形式

| 形式 | 例子 write | 用法 |
|---|---|---|
| 原形 | write | 助动词后、不定式、祈使句 |
| 第三人称单数 | writes | 一般现在时主语为三单 |
| 过去式 | wrote | 一般过去时 |
| 现在分词 / 动名词 | writing | 进行时、非谓语 |
| 过去分词 | written | 完成时、被动、非谓语 |

### 4. 及物、不及物与系动词

| 类型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 及物动词 | verb + object | She opened the file. |
| 不及物动词 | verb | The server crashed. |
| 双宾动词 | verb + indirect object + direct object | He gave me a book. |
| 宾补动词 | verb + object + complement | We found the answer useful. |
| 系动词 | linking verb + complement | The idea sounds good. |

常见系动词：

- be
- seem / appear
- become / get / grow / turn
- look / sound / smell / taste / feel
- remain / stay / keep

### 5. 静态动词与动态动词

静态动词通常不用于进行时，除非表达临时状态、态度变化或特殊含义。

| 静态类别 | 例子 |
|---|---|
| 感知 | see, hear, smell, taste |
| 思维 | know, believe, understand, remember |
| 情感 | like, love, hate, prefer |
| 拥有 | have, own, belong |
| 状态 | be, seem, need, contain |

对比：

- I have a car. 表示拥有，不用进行时。
- I am having lunch. have 表示“吃/进行”，可用进行时。
- I think you are right. think 表示认为。
- I am thinking about the problem. think 表示思考。

## 时态与体

英语时间表达不是只有“过去、现在、将来”，而是 tense + aspect 的组合：

- tense: 现在 / 过去
- aspect: 一般 / 进行 / 完成 / 完成进行
- future: 将来意义通常由 will, be going to, be doing, be to do 等结构表达

### 1. 四种体

| 体 | 核心含义 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| simple | 事实、习惯、整体事件 | I read every day. |
| progressive | 正在进行、临时、未完成 | I am reading now. |
| perfect | 之前发生，对参照时间有影响 | I have read the book. |
| perfect progressive | 从之前持续到参照时间，强调持续 | I have been reading for two hours. |

### 2. 十二种常见时态结构

| 时态 | 结构 | 核心用法 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 一般现在时 | do / does | 习惯、事实、规律、日程 | She writes every morning. |
| 现在进行时 | am/is/are doing | 此刻进行、近期临时安排 | She is writing now. |
| 现在完成时 | have/has done | 过去到现在的结果或经验 | She has written three reports. |
| 现在完成进行时 | have/has been doing | 从过去持续到现在 | She has been writing all day. |
| 一般过去时 | did | 过去某时发生并结束 | She wrote yesterday. |
| 过去进行时 | was/were doing | 过去某时正在进行 | She was writing at 8 p.m. |
| 过去完成时 | had done | 过去的过去 | She had written it before the meeting. |
| 过去完成进行时 | had been doing | 到过去某时前一直在做 | She had been writing for hours before dinner. |
| 一般将来时 | will do | 预测、决定、承诺 | She will write tomorrow. |
| 将来进行时 | will be doing | 将来某时正在进行 | She will be writing at 8 p.m. |
| 将来完成时 | will have done | 到将来某时已完成 | She will have written it by Friday. |
| 将来完成进行时 | will have been doing | 到将来某时持续多久 | She will have been writing for two hours by then. |

### 3. 一般现在时

用途：

- 习惯：I exercise every morning.
- 客观事实：Water boils at 100°C.
- 长期状态：She lives in Shanghai.
- 时间表：The train leaves at 8:30.
- 叙事或说明步骤：First, you open the app.

第三人称单数：

- She works.
- He studies.
- It goes.

### 4. 现在进行时

用途：

- 此刻正在发生：I am reading.
- 近期临时状态：She is staying with friends this week.
- 已安排的近期未来：We are meeting tomorrow.
- 变化趋势：The market is growing.
- 抱怨重复行为：He is always interrupting me.

### 5. 现在完成时

核心：过去发生，但和现在有关。

用途：

- 结果：I have lost my key. 现在没钥匙。
- 经验：She has visited London.
- 持续：They have lived here for ten years.
- 刚刚完成：I have just finished.

常见时间状语：

- already
- yet
- just
- ever
- never
- recently
- so far
- up to now
- for
- since

一般过去时 vs 现在完成时：

| 一般过去时 | 现在完成时 |
|---|---|
| 关注过去具体时间 | 关注现在结果或经验 |
| I saw him yesterday. | I have seen him before. |
| 时间通常结束 | 时间可能延续到现在 |
| She lived here in 2020. | She has lived here since 2020. |

### 6. 现在完成进行时

强调动作从过去持续到现在，常带有“还在继续”或“刚刚停下”的感觉。

- I have been studying grammar for two hours.
- She has been working too hard recently.
- It has been raining all morning.

与现在完成时对比：

- I have read the book. 强调完成结果。
- I have been reading the book. 强调阅读过程和持续。

### 7. 一般过去时

表示过去某个时间发生的动作或状态。

- I met her last week.
- He worked there for five years.
- Did you finish the task?
- She did not agree.

常见时间状语：

- yesterday
- last night
- in 2020
- two days ago
- when I was young

### 8. 过去进行时

用途：

- 过去某时正在进行：I was working at 9.
- 一个动作发生时另一个动作正在进行：I was cooking when he called.
- 背景描写：The wind was blowing and the rain was falling.
- 礼貌表达：I was wondering if you could help.

### 9. 过去完成时

表示“过去的过去”。

- When I arrived, the meeting had started.
- She had never seen snow before she moved north.

如果时间顺序已经很清楚，尤其有 before / after，有时一般过去时也可接受：

- After he finished dinner, he went out.

### 10. 过去完成进行时

强调到过去某个时间点之前，动作一直在持续。

- She had been studying for three hours when I called.
- They had been waiting since morning.

### 11. 将来表达

英语没有单一“将来时”形式，常用多种结构表达将来。

| 结构 | 用法 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| will do | 临时决定、预测、承诺 | I will call you later. |
| be going to do | 计划、明显迹象 | I am going to study abroad. |
| be doing | 已安排的近期计划 | We are leaving tomorrow. |
| simple present | 固定日程 | The flight leaves at 6. |
| be to do | 正式安排、命令 | The president is to visit Paris. |
| be about to do | 即将发生 | The show is about to start. |

will vs be going to：

- I think it will rain. 预测。
- Look at the clouds. It is going to rain. 有迹象。
- I will answer the phone. 临时决定。
- I am going to buy a new laptop. 事先计划。

### 12. 时间和条件从句中的将来

在 when, after, before, until, as soon as, if, unless 等引导的时间或条件从句中，通常用一般现在时表示将来。

- I will call you when I arrive.
- If it rains tomorrow, we will stay home.
- We will start as soon as everyone is ready.

不要说：

- If it will rain tomorrow, we will stay home.  

除非 will 表示意愿：

- If you will wait here, I will get the manager.  
  如果你愿意在这里等。

## 语态语气与情态

### 1. 被动语态

基本结构：

```text
be + past participle
```

例子：

- The file was deleted.
- The issue has been fixed.
- The system is being tested.

被动语态常见时态：

| 时态 | 被动形式 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 一般现在 | am/is/are done | The data is stored locally. |
| 一般过去 | was/were done | The report was published yesterday. |
| 现在进行 | am/is/are being done | The room is being cleaned. |
| 过去进行 | was/were being done | The bridge was being repaired. |
| 现在完成 | have/has been done | The bug has been fixed. |
| 过去完成 | had been done | The file had been removed. |
| 将来 | will be done | The results will be announced. |
| 情态 | modal + be done | The rule should be revised. |

使用被动语态的场景：

1. 不知道动作执行者：My bike was stolen.
2. 执行者不重要：The form must be submitted online.
3. 强调承受者：The decision was approved by the board.
4. 正式、客观文体：The samples were analyzed.

get-passive 更口语，常强调意外或变化：

- He got injured.
- The window got broken.

### 2. 主动与被动转换

主动：

- The engineer fixed the bug.

被动：

- The bug was fixed by the engineer.

不是所有动词都能被动。通常只有及物动词可被动。

### 3. 情态动词

情态动词后接动词原形。

| 情态动词 | 核心含义 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| can | 能力、可能、许可 | She can swim. |
| could | 过去能力、委婉、可能 | Could you help me? |
| may | 许可、可能 | You may leave. |
| might | 较弱可能 | It might rain. |
| must | 必须、强推测 | You must wear a helmet. |
| shall | 建议、正式将来 | Shall we begin? |
| should | 应该、建议、预期 | You should rest. |
| will | 意愿、预测、将来 | I will try. |
| would | 过去将来、委婉、假设 | Would you like tea? |
| need | 需要，半情态 | Need I explain? |
| dare | 敢，半情态 | Dare he ask? |

### 4. 情态意义强弱

可能性：

```text
must > should / ought to > may > might / could
```

例子：

- He must be at home. 几乎确定。
- He should be at home. 按理应该。
- He may be at home. 可能。
- He might be at home. 也许。

义务：

```text
must / have to > should / ought to > had better
```

- You must finish it today. 强义务。
- You have to finish it today. 外部要求。
- You should finish it today. 建议。
- You had better finish it today. 带警告意味。

### 5. 情态动词 + have done

表达对过去的推测、遗憾或批评。

| 结构 | 含义 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| must have done | 过去一定做了 | He must have forgotten. |
| may/might have done | 过去可能做了 | She might have missed the train. |
| cannot/could not have done | 过去不可能做了 | He cannot have said that. |
| should have done | 本该做但没做 | You should have told me. |
| should not have done | 本不该做但做了 | I should not have clicked the link. |
| need not have done | 本不必做但做了 | You need not have waited. |
| would have done | 本会做，常用于虚拟 | I would have helped you. |

### 6. 语气：陈述、祈使、疑问、虚拟

| 语气 | 功能 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 陈述语气 | 陈述事实 | She is here. |
| 疑问语气 | 提问 | Is she here? |
| 祈使语气 | 命令、请求 | Please sit down. |
| 虚拟语气 | 假设、愿望、非事实 | If I were you, I would wait. |

### 7. 虚拟语气

#### 与现在事实相反

```text
If + subject + past tense, subject + would/could/might + verb
```

- If I were you, I would apologize.
- If she had more time, she could learn faster.

be 动词常用 were：

- If he were here, he would know what to do.

#### 与过去事实相反

```text
If + subject + had done, subject + would/could/might have done
```

- If I had known, I would have helped.
- If they had left earlier, they might have caught the train.

#### 与将来可能性很低或假设

- If it rained tomorrow, we would cancel the trip.
- If he were to resign, the team would need a new lead.
- If the system should fail, restart it manually.

#### wish 结构

| 结构 | 含义 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| wish + past tense | 希望现在不同 | I wish I knew the answer. |
| wish + had done | 后悔过去 | I wish I had studied earlier. |
| wish + would do | 希望别人改变 | I wish he would listen. |

#### 建议、要求、命令后的虚拟

在 suggest, recommend, require, demand, insist, propose, order 等词后，that 从句可用动词原形。

- The teacher suggested that he revise the essay.
- It is important that every student be prepared.

美式英语更常用此结构，英式英语也可用 should：

- The teacher suggested that he should revise the essay.

### 8. 条件句总表

| 类型 | 结构 | 含义 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 零条件句 | If + present, present | 普遍规律 | If water freezes, it expands. |
| 第一条件句 | If + present, will do | 真实可能的将来 | If it rains, we will stay home. |
| 第二条件句 | If + past, would do | 现在或将来假设 | If I had time, I would help. |
| 第三条件句 | If + had done, would have done | 过去反事实 | If I had known, I would have called. |
| 混合条件句 | If + had done, would do | 过去影响现在 | If I had studied medicine, I would be a doctor now. |

## 非谓语动词

非谓语动词不是句子的限定谓语，但仍保留动词特征，可带宾语、状语或自己的逻辑主语。

三大类：

| 类型 | 形式 | 核心意义 |
|---|---|---|
| 不定式 | to do | 目的、将来、具体动作 |
| 动名词 / 现在分词 | doing | 名词化、进行、主动 |
| 过去分词 | done | 被动、完成 |

### 1. 不定式

结构：

- to do
- not to do
- to be done
- to have done
- to be doing
- to have been done

功能：

| 功能 | 例句 |
|---|---|
| 主语 | To learn grammar takes time. |
| 宾语 | I want to learn grammar. |
| 表语 | My goal is to write clearly. |
| 定语 | I need a book to read. |
| 状语 | She left early to catch the train. |
| 宾补 | I asked him to wait. |

形式主语更自然：

- It takes time to learn grammar.

### 2. 动名词

doing 作名词用，可作主语、宾语、表语、介词宾语。

- Learning grammar is useful.
- I enjoy reading.
- Her job is editing technical documents.
- He is interested in learning languages.

常接动名词的动词：

- enjoy
- avoid
- finish
- mind
- suggest
- consider
- admit
- deny
- practice
- risk
- keep
- postpone

### 3. 现在分词

doing 作形容词或状语，表示主动、进行。

- The sleeping baby is quiet.
- The woman standing by the door is my teacher.
- Walking down the street, I saw an old friend.

### 4. 过去分词

done 表示被动或完成。

- The broken window needs repair.
- Written in simple English, the book is easy to read.
- The problem discussed yesterday is important.

### 5. 不定式 vs 动名词

| 动词 | 接不定式 | 接动名词 | 区别 |
|---|---|---|---|
| remember | remember to do | remember doing | 记得要做 / 记得做过 |
| forget | forget to do | forget doing | 忘记要做 / 忘记做过 |
| stop | stop to do | stop doing | 停下来去做 / 停止正在做 |
| try | try to do | try doing | 努力做 / 试着做 |
| regret | regret to do | regret doing | 遗憾地要说 / 后悔做过 |
| mean | mean to do | mean doing | 打算做 / 意味着 |

例子：

- I stopped to rest. 我停下来去休息。
- I stopped working. 我停止工作。

### 6. 感官动词和使役动词

常见结构：

| 结构 | 例句 |
|---|---|
| see/hear/watch/notice + object + do | I saw him leave. |
| see/hear/watch/notice + object + doing | I saw him leaving. |
| make/let/have + object + do | The teacher made us rewrite it. |
| get + object + to do | I got him to check the answer. |
| have/get + object + done | I had my hair cut. |

区别：

- I saw him cross the street. 看见全过程。
- I saw him crossing the street. 看见正在过街。

### 7. 独立主格结构

独立主格有自己的逻辑主语，常作状语。

- The weather being fine, we went hiking.
- The meeting over, everyone left.
- He stood there, his hands in his pockets.

书面语中常见，口语中可改成从句：

- Because the weather was fine, we went hiking.

## 句子类型与句子成分

### 1. 按结构分类

| 类型 | 说明 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 简单句 | 一个独立分句 | She writes clearly. |
| 并列句 | 两个或多个独立分句 | She writes clearly, and readers trust her. |
| 复合句 | 一个主句 + 一个或多个从句 | Readers trust her because she writes clearly. |
| 并列复合句 | 并列句中含从句 | She writes clearly, and readers trust her because she is precise. |

### 2. 按功能分类

| 类型 | 功能 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 陈述句 | 陈述 | The system works. |
| 疑问句 | 提问 | Does the system work? |
| 祈使句 | 命令、请求、建议 | Check the logs. |
| 感叹句 | 强烈情感 | What a useful guide it is! |

### 3. 否定句

常见否定词：

- not
- no
- never
- none
- neither
- nor
- hardly
- scarcely
- seldom
- rarely
- little
- few

结构：

- be 动词后加 not：She is not ready.
- 助动词后加 not：She has not finished.
- 实义动词用 do 支撑：She does not agree.
- 情态动词后加 not：You should not wait.

注意否定范围：

- I do not always agree. 我并不总是同意。
- I always do not agree. 不自然；表示总是不同意。

### 4. 疑问句

| 类型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 一般疑问句 | 助动词 / be / 情态 + 主语 | Are you ready? Do you agree? |
| 特殊疑问句 | wh-word + 一般疑问句语序 | What did you see? |
| 选择疑问句 | A or B | Do you want tea or coffee? |
| 反意疑问句 | 陈述 + 简短问句 | You are coming, aren't you? |
| 间接疑问句 | 陈述语序 | I wonder where he lives. |

间接疑问句不要倒装：

- Correct: Do you know where he is?
- Wrong: Do you know where is he?

### 5. 反意疑问句

基本规则：

- 前肯后否：You are ready, aren't you?
- 前否后肯：You are not ready, are you?

特殊情况：

- I am late, aren't I?
- Let's go, shall we?
- Close the door, will you?
- Nobody called, did they?

## 从句系统

从句就是有主谓结构但不能完全独立或在大句中充当成分的结构。

三大从句：

1. 名词性从句：当名词用
2. 定语从句：修饰名词
3. 状语从句：修饰动作、形容词、副词或整句

### 1. 名词性从句

名词性从句可以作主语、宾语、表语、同位语。

| 功能 | 例句 |
|---|---|
| 主语从句 | What he said surprised me. |
| 宾语从句 | I know that he is honest. |
| 表语从句 | The problem is that we lack data. |
| 同位语从句 | The fact that he resigned shocked us. |

引导词：

| 类型 | 引导词 | 说明 |
|---|---|---|
| 陈述意义 | that | 通常不作成分 |
| 是否意义 | whether, if | whether 更正式，可接 or not |
| 疑问意义 | who, what, which, when, where, why, how | 在从句中作成分或状语 |

例子：

- That she passed the exam is good news.
- Whether he will come is uncertain.
- I do not know what she wants.
- The question is how we can improve it.

形式主语 it：

- It is clear that he made a mistake.
- It remains unknown whether the plan will work.

### 2. 宾语从句

语序用陈述语序：

- I know where he lives.
- She asked why the system failed.

时态呼应：

- He says that he is tired.
- He said that he was tired.

如果从句是客观真理，不随主句过去而改变：

- The teacher said that water boils at 100°C.

### 3. 同位语从句 vs 定语从句

同位语从句解释名词内容，that 不作成分：

- The news that he won the prize is true.

定语从句修饰名词，that 作成分：

- The news that he told me is true.

判断方法：如果从句等于名词的内容，是同位语；如果从句描述这个名词是哪一个，是定语。

### 4. 定语从句

定语从句修饰名词或代词。

结构：

```text
先行词 + 关系词 + 从句
```

例子：

- The student who asked the question is my friend.
- The book that I bought yesterday is useful.
- The city where I grew up has changed a lot.

### 5. 关系词选择

| 先行词 / 功能 | 主语 | 宾语 | 所有格 | 状语 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 人 | who / that | who / whom / that | whose | - |
| 物 | which / that | which / that | whose / of which | - |
| 地点 | - | - | - | where |
| 时间 | - | - | - | when |
| 原因 | - | - | - | why |

例子：

- The engineer who fixed the bug is here.
- The bug that she fixed was serious.
- The student whose essay won is absent.
- The room where we met was small.
- The day when we met was rainy.
- The reason why he left is unclear.

### 6. 限制性与非限制性定语从句

| 类型 | 功能 | 标点 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 限制性 | 限定是哪一个 | 不用逗号 | The students who study hard improve quickly. |
| 非限制性 | 补充说明 | 用逗号 | My brother, who lives in Beijing, is a doctor. |

区别：

- My brother who lives in Beijing is a doctor.  
  我有多个兄弟，住北京的那个是医生。
- My brother, who lives in Beijing, is a doctor.  
  我哥哥是医生，补充说明他住北京。

非限制性定语从句通常不用 that：

- Correct: The plan, which was risky, succeeded.
- Wrong: The plan, that was risky, succeeded.

### 7. 关系词省略

关系词在限制性定语从句中作宾语时可省略：

- The book that I bought is useful.
- The book I bought is useful.

作主语不能省略：

- The student who asked the question is my friend.

### 8. 介词 + 关系代词

正式：

- The person to whom I spoke was kind.
- The topic about which we argued was complex.

自然口语：

- The person who I spoke to was kind.
- The topic that we argued about was complex.

### 9. 定语从句简化

主动进行：

- The man who is standing there is my teacher.
- The man standing there is my teacher.

被动：

- The report that was written yesterday is important.
- The report written yesterday is important.

形容词短语：

- Anyone who is interested in the course can register.
- Anyone interested in the course can register.

### 10. 状语从句

状语从句说明时间、原因、条件、让步、目的、结果、比较、方式、地点等。

| 类型 | 引导词 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 时间 | when, while, before, after, until, since, as soon as | Call me when you arrive. |
| 原因 | because, since, as | I stayed home because it rained. |
| 条件 | if, unless, as long as, provided that | If you practice, you will improve. |
| 让步 | although, though, even though, while | Although it was hard, she continued. |
| 目的 | so that, in order that | Speak clearly so that everyone can hear. |
| 结果 | so ... that, such ... that | It was so cold that we stayed inside. |
| 比较 | than, as ... as | She writes better than I do. |
| 方式 | as, as if, as though | Do as I say. |
| 地点 | where, wherever | Sit where you like. |

### 11. 状语从句的位置

从句在前通常加逗号：

- If it rains, we will stay home.

从句在后通常不用逗号：

- We will stay home if it rains.

让步从句、插入性从句或较长从句可根据语气使用逗号。

### 12. because / since / as / for

| 词 | 用法 |
|---|---|
| because | 直接原因，语气最明确 |
| since | 既然，原因已知 |
| as | 由于，语气较弱 |
| for | 并列连词，正式，补充解释 |

例子：

- I left because I was tired.
- Since everyone is here, let's begin.
- As it was late, we went home.
- I stayed home, for I felt ill.

### 13. although / though / even though / while

- Although it was raining, we went out.
- Though it was raining, we went out.
- Even though it was raining, we went out. 语气更强。
- While I agree with the goal, I question the method. 表对比让步。

不要同时使用 although 和 but：

- Correct: Although it was raining, we went out.
- Correct: It was raining, but we went out.
- Wrong: Although it was raining, but we went out.

## 特殊句式

### 1. 倒装

倒装分为全部倒装和部分倒装。

#### 全部倒装

整个谓语放在主语前，常用于地点或方向副词开头。

- Here comes the bus.
- On the wall hung a painting.
- Away ran the children.

主语是代词时通常不倒装：

- Here it comes.

#### 部分倒装

助动词、be 或情态动词放在主语前。

否定或半否定词位于句首：

- Never have I seen such a result.
- Rarely does he complain.
- Hardly had we arrived when it began to rain.
- Not only did she pass, but she also won a prize.

only + 状语位于句首：

- Only then did I understand the problem.
- Only by practicing can you improve.

so / neither / nor：

- She likes grammar, and so do I.
- He cannot swim, and neither can I.

虚拟条件句省略 if：

- Had I known, I would have helped.
- Were I you, I would wait.
- Should you need help, call me.

### 2. 强调句

结构：

```text
It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who + 其他
```

例子：

- It was John who fixed the bug.
- It was yesterday that I met her.
- It is clarity that matters most.

判断方法：去掉 It is/was 和 that/who 后，句子仍基本完整。

### 3. do 强调

用 do / does / did 强调谓语。

- I do understand.
- She does care.
- He did finish the task.

### 4. cleft 与 pseudo-cleft

It-cleft：

- It was the design that caused the delay.

Wh-cleft：

- What caused the delay was the design.

用途：突出焦点信息。

### 5. 祈使句

基本形式用动词原形：

- Open the file.
- Please wait here.
- Do not touch the wire.
- Let's begin.

带主语的祈使句：

- You be careful.
- Everybody sit down.

### 6. 感叹句

| 结构 | 例句 |
|---|---|
| What + a/an + adjective + noun + subject + verb | What a clear answer it is! |
| What + adjective + plural/uncountable noun | What useful advice! |
| How + adjective/adverb + subject + verb | How clearly she explains! |

### 7. 间接引语

直接引语：

- He said, "I am tired."

间接引语：

- He said that he was tired.

常见变化：

| 直接 | 间接 |
|---|---|
| am/is | was |
| are | were |
| do/does | did |
| did | had done |
| will | would |
| can | could |
| may | might |
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| yesterday | the day before |
| tomorrow | the next day |
| here | there |
| this | that |

客观真理不变：

- The teacher said that the earth moves around the sun.

### 8. 主谓分隔

长句中主语和谓语之间可能插入定语、介词短语或从句，不要被迷惑。

- The list of problems that the team found during testing is long.

主语核心是 list，所以谓语是 is。

## 一致替代省略与平行

### 1. 主谓一致

基本原则：谓语动词与主语核心名词一致。

| 情况 | 规则 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 单数主语 | 单数谓语 | The report is ready. |
| 复数主语 | 复数谓语 | The reports are ready. |
| and 连接 | 通常复数 | Tom and Jerry are friends. |
| each/every/either/neither | 通常单数 | Each student has a book. |
| 集合名词 | 看作整体用单数，看作成员用复数 | The team is strong. The team are arguing. |
| 不定代词 | many 复数，much 单数 | Many were invited. Much was lost. |
| 距离主语远 | 看主语核心，不看近处名词 | The quality of the products is high. |

### 2. 就近原则

there be, either ... or, neither ... nor, not only ... but also 等常按靠近谓语的名词决定。

- There is a book and two pens.
- There are two pens and a book.
- Either you or he is responsible.
- Neither he nor they are ready.

### 3. 代词一致

代词要与先行词在人称、数、性或语义上保持一致。

- Every student should bring his or her book.
- Every student should bring their book.  
  现代英语中 singular they 很常见，尤其性别未知时。

### 4. 替代

英语用替代避免重复。

| 替代词 | 替代对象 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| one / ones | 可数名词 | I need a pen. Do you have one? |
| do / does / did | 动词短语 | She works harder than I do. |
| so | 前文内容 | I think so. |
| not | 否定前文内容 | I hope not. |
| the same | 相同事物 | Mine is the same. |

### 5. 省略

省略是省去上下文能恢复的信息。

- She can play the piano, and he can too.
- If possible, send the file today.
- When in doubt, check the source.
- The sooner, the better.

### 6. 平行结构

并列成分应保持语法形式一致。

好：

- The course teaches reading, writing, and speaking.
- She wants to learn grammar, to improve writing, and to speak confidently.

差：

- The course teaches reading, writing, and how to speak.
- She wants to learn grammar, improving writing, and to speak confidently.

## 标点大小写与书面规范

### 1. 逗号

常见用法：

1. 连接较长并列句时，放在并列连词前：
   - The rule is simple, but the usage is difficult.
2. 从句在主句前：
   - If you need help, ask me.
3. 非限制性定语从句：
   - My teacher, who lives nearby, is kind.
4. 插入语：
   - This method, however, is not perfect.
5. 列举：
   - We need time, money, and data.

不要用逗号连接两个完整句，这叫 comma splice：

- Wrong: I was tired, I went home.
- Correct: I was tired, so I went home.
- Correct: I was tired; I went home.
- Correct: Because I was tired, I went home.

### 2. 分号

连接关系紧密的两个独立分句。

- The rule is simple; the usage is subtle.
- Some students prefer examples; others prefer rules.

也可用于复杂列表：

- The team includes Alice, the editor; Bob, the designer; and Chen, the engineer.

### 3. 冒号

引出解释、列表、总结。

- We need three things: time, data, and patience.
- The conclusion is clear: the test must be repeated.

### 4. 破折号

表示插入、转折、补充，语气较强。

- The answer is simple: practice.
- The answer is simple - practice.

在正式学术写作中不要滥用。

### 5. 引号

美式英语通常句号和逗号放在引号内：

- She said, "I agree."

英式英语更常按逻辑位置处理。保持同一文档风格一致。

### 6. 大小写

大写：

- 句首
- 人名、地名、组织名
- 星期、月份、节日
- 语言、国籍
- 标题中的主要词，视风格而定

例子：

- She studies English on Monday.
- The Spring Festival is important in China.

## 篇章语法与信息组织

### 1. 已知信息与新信息

英语句子通常先放读者已知或主题信息，再引入新信息。

- The old version was slow. The new version is much faster.

不要随意跳主题。清晰写作依赖稳定的主语链。

### 2. 连接手段

| 关系 | 连接词 |
|---|---|
| 添加 | moreover, furthermore, in addition, also |
| 转折 | however, nevertheless, yet, still |
| 原因 | because, since, as, due to |
| 结果 | therefore, thus, as a result, consequently |
| 对比 | in contrast, on the other hand, whereas |
| 举例 | for example, for instance, such as |
| 总结 | in short, overall, in conclusion |
| 顺序 | first, next, then, finally |

连接副词不能像并列连词一样直接用逗号连接两个句子：

- Wrong: The test failed, therefore we revised the code.
- Correct: The test failed; therefore, we revised the code.
- Correct: The test failed, so we revised the code.

### 3. 指代清晰

代词必须让读者知道指谁或什么。

不清楚：

- When the file was moved to the folder, it disappeared.

it 可能指 file 或 folder。改写：

- The file disappeared after it was moved to the folder.

### 4. 信息重点

英语通过位置、强调句、被动语态、cleft 结构等表达重点。

普通：

- The engineer fixed the bug yesterday.

强调人：

- It was the engineer who fixed the bug yesterday.

强调时间：

- It was yesterday that the engineer fixed the bug.

强调对象：

- The bug was fixed yesterday.

### 5. 正式与非正式语法

| 非正式 | 正式 |
|---|---|
| a lot of | many / much / numerous |
| kids | children |
| get | obtain / become / receive，视语境 |
| can't | cannot |
| lots of stuff | many factors / much information |
| start | begin / initiate，视语境 |

正式不等于复杂。优秀的正式英语通常是准确、清楚、克制。

## 中国学习者高频易错点

### 1. 冠词缺失

错误：

- I bought book.

正确：

- I bought a book.
- I bought the book.
- I bought this book.

单数可数名词通常需要限定词。

### 2. 名词单复数

错误：

- I have many information.

正确：

- I have a lot of information.
- I have many pieces of information.

### 3. 第三人称单数

错误：

- She work hard.

正确：

- She works hard.

### 4. 时态只按中文时间词翻译

中文“我学英语三年了”不是：

- I learned English for three years.

如果现在还在学：

- I have learned English for three years.
- I have been learning English for three years.

### 5. although 与 but 连用

错误：

- Although it was hard, but I finished it.

正确：

- Although it was hard, I finished it.
- It was hard, but I finished it.

### 6. because 与 so 连用

错误：

- Because I was tired, so I went home.

正确：

- Because I was tired, I went home.
- I was tired, so I went home.

### 7. 形容词和副词混用

错误：

- She speaks English good.

正确：

- She speaks English well.
- Her English is good.

### 8. 介词直译

错误：

- discuss about the issue

正确：

- discuss the issue
- talk about the issue

错误：

- enter into the room

正确：

- enter the room
- go into the room

### 9. 宾语从句语序

错误：

- I do not know where is he.

正确：

- I do not know where he is.

### 10. 定语从句缺成分或重复成分

错误：

- The book which I bought it is useful.

正确：

- The book which I bought is useful.
- The book I bought is useful.

### 11. 主谓不一致

错误：

- The quality of the products are high.

正确：

- The quality of the products is high.

主语核心是 quality。

### 12. run-on sentence

错误：

- I was tired I went home.

正确：

- I was tired, so I went home.
- I was tired. I went home.
- Because I was tired, I went home.

### 13. very + 动词

错误：

- I very like English.

正确：

- I like English very much.
- I really like English.

### 14. there be 与 have 混用

表达“某地有某物”：

- There is a book on the desk.

表达“某人拥有”：

- I have a book.

中文“有”要按意义区分。

### 15. 过度使用被动或名词化

笨重：

- The implementation of the solution was conducted by the team.

清楚：

- The team implemented the solution.

被动语态有用，但不要为了显得正式而牺牲清晰。

## 学习路线与练习方法

### 1. 三阶段路线

#### 第一阶段：句子骨架

目标：

- 能识别主语、谓语、宾语、表语、补语
- 能写五大基本句型
- 能正确使用名词单复数、冠词、一般时态

练习：

- 每天拆 10 个句子主干。
- 用五大句型各造 5 句。
- 改错：冠词、三单、复数、基本时态。

#### 第二阶段：动词和从句

目标：

- 能区分 12 种时态的核心意义
- 能使用被动、情态、虚拟语气
- 能写名词性从句、定语从句、状语从句

练习：

- 把简单句合并为复合句。
- 把主动句改为被动句，再判断是否自然。
- 用同一动词写不同时态例句。

#### 第三阶段：篇章和风格

目标：

- 能写清楚的段落
- 能使用连接词而不堆砌
- 能控制句子长度、重点和正式程度

练习：

- 每天改写一段中文式英语。
- 给段落标出 topic sentence, supporting details, conclusion。
- 检查代词指代、平行结构、标点。

### 2. 句子分析模板

```text
Sentence:

1. Main verb:
2. Subject:
3. Object / complement:
4. Modifiers:
5. Clauses:
6. Grammar focus:
7. Possible rewrite:
```

示例：

```text
Sentence:
The app that we launched last month has attracted users who need a simple way to manage notes.

1. Main verb: has attracted
2. Subject: The app
3. Object: users
4. Modifiers: that we launched last month; who need a simple way to manage notes
5. Clauses: two relative clauses
6. Grammar focus: present perfect + relative clauses
7. Possible rewrite: Since its launch last month, the app has attracted users who need simple note management.
```

### 3. 写作自查清单

写完英文后，按顺序检查：

1. 每个句子是否有一个清楚的限定谓语？
2. 主语和谓语是否一致？
3. 单数可数名词是否有冠词或其他限定词？
4. 时态是否符合时间和语境？
5. 代词指代是否清楚？
6. 从句是否完整，关系词是否重复或缺失？
7. 并列结构是否平行？
8. 是否有逗号拼接或流水句？
9. 介词搭配是否自然？
10. 句子是否可以更短、更直接？
